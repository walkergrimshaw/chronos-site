# Chronos

**Smart task scheduling for Mac.**

Chronos connects to your Google Calendar and automatically schedules your tasks around existing commitments. Tell it what you need to do, how long it'll take, and when it's due — Chronos handles the rest.

---

## How It Works

1. **Add your tasks** — name, deadline, estimated hours, and priority.
2. **Connect Google Calendar** — Chronos reads your existing events to find open time.
3. **Auto-schedule** — tasks are placed into available slots, respecting your availability windows and priorities.
4. **Stay flexible** — drag, reschedule, or reshuffle anytime. Chronos adapts.

---

## Key Features

- **Google Calendar integration** — reads your schedule, writes task blocks
- **Priority-aware scheduling** — urgent work gets scheduled first
- **Recurring tasks** — daily, weekly, or custom recurrence
- **Availability windows** — only schedule during your working hours
- **Local-first** — your data stays on your Mac, syncs via iCloud or any folder
- **No account required** — just your Google Calendar sign-in

---
